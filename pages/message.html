    What Director Says

    - Mrs. Chinu Gurung

    Director

    My name is Chinu Gurung with over two decade’s experience as a beautician & make up artist, you can understand that this is really my passion. Beauty treatment,make up & beauty advice are all very personal and need to be customized for the customer . Now a days, more than ever, it is important to have a beauty salon where personal care is important with professional advice & a place to find real relaxation … Beauty sector is really a big passion of mine. So finally in January 1996 AD, I decided to start my own salon & use all the experience & gained to implement them with my own philosophy. Where I think a visit to Gurung Beauty Parlour & Beauty Academy P.Ltd should be a complete beauty experience. We strive to ,make your experience unique & very enjoyable & are always appreciative of any comments or thoughts you have & would like to share with us !
    It is our immense pleasure to welcome you all at Gurung Beauty Parlour & Academy where our team of experienced and professionally trained to give you best services. We pride ourselves on being the best from the latest beauty treatment to highly trained person. We are truly dedicated to our customer to “Come feel the difference” at our place. We also provide the best learning courses according to CTEVT courses.
    Our goal is to provide first class customer service to all clients, unbeatable cleanliness and most importantly, convenience. We offer you a learning experience in intimate surroundings that we hope will stimulate and inspire you.
    Our aim is to give our costumers a variety of treatments that provide great results & great value for money. First & foremost as & aim to satisfy each & every clients that visits our salon. I am delighted to be providing my full range of beauty treatments to clients.
    I assure that Gurung Beauty Parlour and Academy with its committed founders with professionally trained and dedicated staff helps you to remain worry free about your looks.
